Special Dates (holidays, Ramadan, exams…)

On a special date you can turn off all automatic alerts, or run a different timetable just for that day. This replaces the weekly schedule for that date only. Remember to press Save schedule after editing.

Connect the other computers

On every other computer, open a browser and go to the address shown in the server window (looks like http://192.168.x.x:3000/). Then tap “Enable sound” once on each screen. Alerts then arrive automatically. Use Open a Display (bottom-left) to preview a screen.
